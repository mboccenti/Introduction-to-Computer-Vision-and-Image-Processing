Task 2: Training Your Classifier

To get access to the model, a Train run has been created for you. Click on Open Notebook.

You can also create your own Train run:

  1. Click on Train Model on the side panel and
  2. Create a New training run, enter a Name, choose Jupyter notebook as a "Training tool", and select Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) with PyTorch.

Make sure you run the notebook to the end to ensure that the model is saved to CV Studio.
